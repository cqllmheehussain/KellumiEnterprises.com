Welcome to Kellumi Enterprises

AJI KELLUMI GENERAL ENTERPRISES LIMITED, trading as Kellumi Enterprises LTD, is a diversified business based in Maiduguri, Borno State, Nigeria. We specialize in multiple sectors including retail, technology, and education.

Our mission is to provide high-quality products and services that meet the needs of our customers while maintaining competitive pricing and exceptional customer service.

With years of experience in the market, we have built a reputation for reliability and excellence in all our business ventures.
